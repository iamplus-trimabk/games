🐾 Animal Word Dodge 🐾

Don't pick the same word as the Denner!

Perfect for ages 5-7

🎯 Game Setup 🎯

📖 How to Play:

1. One player is the "Denner" who secretly picks a word

2. All other players choose from the same list of words

3. If you pick the same word as the Denner, you're OUT!

4. Last player remaining wins and becomes the next Denner

5. Points: 1st out = 0 points, 2nd out = 1 point, etc.
